# Privacy Policy — River Navigator

_Last updated: 13 June 2026_

River Navigator ("the app") is a river navigation app. This policy explains what
data the app handles. The short version: the app does **not** collect, store, or
transmit your personal data to the developer.

## Data the app uses

**Location.** The app uses your device location (including in the background while
you navigate) to show your position on the route, calculate how far is left, and
measure distance to points you mark. Your location is processed **only on your
device**. It is **not** sent to the developer and is **not** shared with any third
party for advertising or analytics.

**Routes and notes.** Routes you import (GPX files) and notes you create are stored
**locally on your device** only. They are not uploaded anywhere.

**Map tiles.** To display maps and to download maps for offline use, the app
requests map tiles from **OpenFreeMap** (https://openfreemap.org). These requests
necessarily include the geographic area of the map being shown or downloaded. The
app does not send OpenFreeMap any account, identity, or personal information.

## What the app does NOT do

- No user accounts or sign-in
- No analytics or usage tracking
- No advertising
- No selling or sharing of personal data

## Permissions

- **Location (When In Use / Always):** required to show your position during
  navigation and to keep the route updated while the screen is off. You can change
  this at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services.

## Children

The app is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect data from anyone.
